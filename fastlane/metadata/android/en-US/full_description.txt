Rouse Context turns your Android phone into an on-demand
[MCP](https://modelcontextprotocol.io/) (Model Context Protocol) server. AI
clients connect to a URL, your phone wakes up, and a direct encrypted session is
established. The AI asks for what it needs — health data, notifications, app
usage — your phone responds, then it goes back to sleep. Your data never leaves
the device except through that live, user-approved session.

This is the FOSS build: it wakes via [UnifiedPush](https://unifiedpush.org/)
instead of Firebase Cloud Messaging and links no Google Play Services or Firebase
code. To be woken while the app is in the background, install a UnifiedPush
distributor (for example ntfy).

Features:

* On-demand — your phone is only reachable while an AI client is connected, then
  goes back to sleep. No always-on background service draining the battery.
* You stay in control — every integration is one you explicitly enable, and each
  tool call is recorded in an on-device audit log you can review.
* End-to-end encrypted — sessions run directly between the AI client and your
  device; the relay only forwards encrypted traffic and cannot read it.
* Google-free — UnifiedPush wake-ups, a hardware-backed device keypair for
  identity, and ACRA crash reporting. Zero Firebase or Play Services.
